Installation


Installations

We employ our own forces in the field to install the products we manufacture. Our installers are qualified journeymen glaziers (Architectural Glass & Metal Technicians). The majority of our installation staff has been with us for many years. This enables us to maintain quality and consistency from project to project. Sub-contract erection crews used by many of our competitors cannot obtain the same quality and consistency. We are always looking to improve our field installation methods. Whenever possible we utilize equipment and machinery to install our products in a pre-assembled or pre-glazed condition.
